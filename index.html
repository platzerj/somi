Ali se vam gel nikoli ne prime pravilno?

Does your gel never hold the way it should?

Poznate ta občutek. Komaj teden dni je za vami, pa se gel že lušči. Nohti se lomijo, kožica nikoli ne izgleda urejena. Hodili ste iz salona v salon in se spraševali, ali so krhki nohti preprosto vaša usoda. Pravi razlog je preprost: povsod ista rešitev za vse, nikoli prav za vaše nohte.

You know the feeling. Barely a week behind you, and the gel is already peeling. Your nails break, the cuticles never look cared for. You went from salon to salon, wondering whether brittle nails are simply your lot. The real reason is simple: the same solution for everyone, never one made for your nails.

V studiu SOMI delam drugače. Najprej analiziram tip vaše nohtne plošče in šele nato izberem material in tehniko, ki mu ustrezata. Ker prava tehnika za vaš noht je tista, ki ne odstopi. In prav zato rezultat tokrat zdrži.

At SOMI, I work differently. First I analyse your nail plate type, and only then choose the material and technique that suit it. Because the right technique for your nail is the one that does not lift. And that is exactly why the result holds this time.

Vaši nohti v dobrih rokah

Your nails in good hands

Verjamem, da so zdravi nohti osnova vsega lepega. Zato pri svojem delu vedno postavim zdravje nohtne plošče na prvo mesto in ne hitim s postopki, ki bi jih poškodovali.

I believe healthy nails are the foundation of everything beautiful. That is why I always put the health of your nail plate first and never rush through steps that could cause damage.

Vsako stranko obravnavam individualno. Preden se lotimo barve ali oblike, najprej raziščemo, kakšen je vaš tip nohtov in kaj potrebujete. Le tako zagotovim rezultat, ki resnično vzdrži in izgleda naravno lepo.

I treat every client individually. Before we even think about colour or shape, we explore your nail type and what you actually need. Only then can I deliver a result that truly lasts and looks naturally beautiful.

Delam brez TPO, saj mi je vaše zdravje pri srcu. Materiali, ki jih uporabljam, so skrbno izbrani, varni in preverjeni. Vsa orodja so sterilizirana med vsakim obiskom.

I work without TPO because your health matters to me. The materials I use are carefully selected, safe and tested. All tools are sterilised between every appointment.

Pogosta vprašanja FAQ

Imate vprašanje? Tu je odgovor.

Have a question? Here's your answer.

BIAB je primeren za krhke nohte, saj jih utrdi. Gel lak je običajen trajni lak, ki ne utrjuje nohtov.
BIAB is suited to brittle nails, as it strengthens them. Gel polish is a regular long-lasting polish that does not strengthen the nails.
To je zelo odvisno od vaših naravnih nohtov. Na splošno moje stranke nosijo gel lak od 2 do 4 tednov.
It depends a lot on your natural nails. In general, my clients wear gel polish for 2 to 4 weeks.
Če vaša manikerka pravilno neguje vaše nohte, bodo zdravi.
If your nail technician cares for your nails properly, they will stay healthy.
Skrbim za zdravje vaših nohtov in uporabljam izključno profesionalne gele znamke Kinetics, ki ne vsebujejo TPO.
I take care of the health of your nails and use only professional Kinetics gels, which are TPO-free.
Vsi instrumenti so podvrženi dvojnemu postopku obdelave: dezinfekciji in sterilizaciji. Prva stopnja je dezinfekcija v posebnem raztopilu, druga stopnja pa sterilizacija s vročim zrakom v kraftovih vrečkah.
All instruments go through a two-step treatment process: disinfection and sterilisation. The first step is disinfection in a special solution, the second is hot-air sterilisation in kraft pouches.
Japonska manikura je naravna nega brez laka: nohti se polirajo s posebno pasto s čebeljim voskom in minerali, da se svetijo in okrepijo. Namenjena je tistim, ki želijo zdrave, negovane nohte brez prekrivanja, še posebej po podaljševanju ali pri krhkih nohtih.
A Japanese manicure is a natural, polish-free treatment: the nails are buffed with a special paste of beeswax and minerals so they shine and grow stronger. It is for anyone who wants healthy, well-groomed nails without an overlay, especially after extensions or for brittle nails.
Popravila nohtov so brezplačna, če je škodo povzročila manikerka (nepravilno izbrani materiali za določen tip nohtov, neustrezna tehnika nanašanja). Če je škodo povzročila stranka (mehanska ali kemična poškodba), bo manikerka za popravilo zaračunala pristojbino. Nohtov ni mogoče popraviti po 2,5-3 tednih nošenja, saj je to standardna življenjska doba gel manikure.
Nail repairs are provided free of charge if the damage was caused by the technician (incorrectly selected materials for the nail type, improper application technique). If the damage was caused by the client (mechanical or chemical damage), the technician will charge a fee for the repair. Nails cannot be repaired after 2.5-3 weeks, as this is the standard lifespan of a gel manicure.
