O meni About Me

Mariia Ivanova

Vaša nehtna strokovnjakinja

Your nail care specialist

Verjamem, da so zdravi nohti osnova vsega lepega. Zato pri svojem delu vedno postavim zdravje nohtne plošče na prvo mesto in ne hitim s postopki, ki bi jih poškodovali.

I believe healthy nails are the foundation of everything beautiful. That is why I always put the health of your nail plate first and never rush through steps that could cause damage.

Vsako stranko obravnavam individualno. Preden se lotimo barve ali oblike, najprej raziščemo, kakšen je vaš tip nohtov in kaj potrebujete. Le tako zagotovim rezultat, ki resnično vzdrži in izgleda naravno lepo.

I treat every client individually. Before we even think about colour or shape, we explore your nail type and what you actually need. Only then can I deliver a result that truly lasts and looks naturally beautiful.

Delam brez TPO, saj mi je vaše zdravje pri srcu. Materiali, ki jih uporabljam, so skrbno izbrani, varni in preverjeni. Vsa orodja so sterilizirana med vsakim obiskom.

I work without MMA because your health matters to me. The materials I use are carefully selected, safe and tested. All tools are sterilised between every appointment.

Pogosta vprašanja FAQ

Imate vprašanje? Tu je odgovor.

Have a question? Here's your answer.

Gel je trden material, ki zagotavlja moč in podaljšanje nohtov, ter drži do 3,5 tedna. BIAB (Builder In A Bottle) je tekoči graditeljski gel, nанesen s čopičem, idealen za tanke ali krhke nohte z naravnejšim videzom.
Hard gel is a rigid material that adds strength and length, lasting up to 3.5 weeks. BIAB (Builder In A Bottle) is a brush-applied builder gel, ideal for thin or brittle nails that need a natural look with a little extra structure.
Japonska ali navadna manikura traja okoli 45 minut. Permanentno lakiranje potrebuje ~2 uri, z dodatki pa 2 uri in 15 minut. Podaljševanje nohtov traja 2 uri. Vedno si vzamem čas, ki ga vaši nohti potrebujejo.
A Japanese or classic manicure takes around 45 minutes. Gel polish needs approximately 2 hours, with add-ons around 2 hours 15 minutes. Extensions take 2 hours. I always take as long as your nails need.
Stopite v stik z mano čim prej. Majhno popravilo do 3 nohtov je na voljo za 10 €. Skupaj bova ugotovili, kaj je najboljša rešitev za naslednjič.
Contact me as soon as possible. A small repair of up to 3 nails is available for 10 €. Together we will work out the best solution going forward.

I work exclusively with quality, MMA-free materials. I select rubber base or BIAB based on your nail plate type. The Japanese manicure pastes contain beeswax, royal jelly, panthenol and vitamins A and E.
